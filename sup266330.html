SUP266330

02 Mar 2019 | 611 字 | 2 分钟读完

This site was stopped playing at 20:36 in Feb 28. They are running 4.18.1.414.

We find DJ changed mode from AUTO to LIVE_ASSIST at 20:35:36. Logevent#1421706 should begin to play at 20:36:00, there are only 24 seconds to assign sreams for itself. But I find there was no stream assigned to it, but the sequencer was still in LIVE_ASSIST mode, sequencer couldn’t decide to play which event in the next, so it was waiting(my prediction). There are 6 streams assigned to sequencer play container and the assignment type is always sequential in this station settings. I use the same settings in my database, can not reproduce it, sequencer play well even I change the mode while the live event is fired to play in few seconds.
